6.3 Quality Assurance Protocol

Escalation Rules:
  • Uncertainty > 0.7: Senior pathologist review (highly uncertain)
  • Uncertainty 0.4-0.7: Standard pathologist review (moderate uncertainty)
  • Uncertainty < 0.4: AI decision (confident predictions)
  • Emergency/Critical: Always human review regardless of uncertainty

9. Limitations & Future Work

9.1 Current Limitations

‚ö† Important Limitations:
  • Single Dataset: Evaluated on PathMNIST only; generalization to other pathology types unknown
  • Assumed Human Accuracy: Used 95% assumption; actual human performance not measured
  • Binary Deferral: Simple threshold approach; could use more sophisticated strategies
  • No Ensemble Methods: Single model; ensemble could improve uncertainty estimates
  • Temporal Stability: No evaluation of model drift or degradation over time

9.2 Future Research Directions

Recommended Next Steps:
  • Multi-Dataset Validation: Test on ChestMNIST, DermaMNIST, BloodMNIST
  • Ensemble Methods: Combine multiple architectures for better uncertainty
  • Human Studies: Collect actual human review decisions and feedback
  • Adaptive Thresholds: Dynamic threshold adjustment based on human accuracy
  • Advanced Uncertainty: Deep ensembles, MC Dropout, Bayesian approaches
  • Active Learning: Use triage data to improve model training
